Accessibility — designed to AAA contrast

  • Body text on navy backgrounds uses cream (#EEDAB3), which passes WCAG AAA at all sizes.
  • Body text on parchment / cream uses dark navy (#0A2240) or warm dark (#2c2a25), passing AAA.
  • Gold (#FFD040) on navy is used only for headings and large text (14pt+ bold), which passes AA Large / AAA Large.
  • Visible focus states on every interactive element (3px gold outline, 3px offset).
  • Semantic HTML, skip link, proper heading hierarchy, alt text on all imagery.

Imagery — from your library

Both pages use photography from the site's `/images` folder: Bishop Siegel, the cathedral interior, warm parish scenes, and article thumbnails. Swap any of them; the layout holds the new image.

What the client is choosing

  • Direction A: how the marquee pages (home, Our Bishop, Give) should feel.
  • Direction B: how the content-heavy pages (sacraments, prayer, long-form articles) should feel.
  • Most likely outcome: both directions live together — A for hero/brand moments, B for editorial depth.
