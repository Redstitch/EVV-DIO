Mission Statement of the Diocese of Evansville

We, the Catholics of the Diocese of Evansville, are committed to continuing the mission Jesus entrusted to all the baptized — to joyfully proclaim the Gospel, serve those in need, and form disciples by inviting everyone to Encounter, Deepen, and Engage.
