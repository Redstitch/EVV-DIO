Plan of Action

Diocese of Evansville — Website Consolidation & Redesign
Prepared: March 2026 | Version 1.0

Executive Summary

This plan outlines the consolidation of evdio.org (177 existing pages) and evdiomessage.org into a single, modern website built on Redstitch's headless CMS architecture. The new consolidated site will contain approximately 160+ English pages (320+ with Spanish translation), including 45 individual parish pages, 35+ ministry pages, and a full integrated news platform for The Message. The site will reorganize content around visitor needs (not departmental structure) and deliver an accessible, bilingual experience that tells the Diocese's story.

The project follows a phased approach: Discovery and resolution of open items, followed by design, content development, build, and a managed launch with an intentional transition period where the old site remains accessible.

Guiding Principles: Tell our story. Meet people where they are. Be ambassadors of one another. Encounter. Deepen. Engage.

Risk Assessment & Mitigation

RiskLikelihoodImpactMitigation
Open items not resolved promptly by Diocese Medium High Clear deadlines per item; weekly check-ins; identify single decision-maker (Dean Happe)
Content review delays from Diocese Medium Medium Batch content for review; set 5-business-day review windows; escalation path
Parish data incomplete or inaccurate Medium Medium Request data early in Phase 1; validate with Diocese staff; plan for iterative corrections
Scope creep from department heads High Medium Clearly documented scope boundaries; changes go through Dean Happe; change request process
Spanish translation quality issues Low Medium Diocese Hispanic Ministry review; professional translation services; iterative review
PDF migration volume larger than expected Medium Low Early audit in Phase 1; automated migration tools; prioritize essential documents
Old site dependency post-launch Medium Low Transition period planned; old site access maintained; clear sunset timeline
