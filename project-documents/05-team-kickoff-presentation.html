Discovery Findings

Problems We're Solving

  • Department-centric navigation — site is organized how the diocese works internally, not how visitors think
  • Overwhelming menus — 16+ top-level items. A newcomer has no idea where to start
  • Invisible storytelling — great ministry work and video content, but the impact doesn't show up on the site
  • Giving has no context — donate button goes off-site with zero narrative or impact stories
  • Two separate websites — The Message journalism lives on a separate site and isn't leveraged on the main presence
  • No site search — required content (Safe Environment, allegations) can't be found without prominent placement
  • Inconsistent upkeep — 16 department logins but only a few update regularly
  • Weak mobile experience — basic responsive with a single breakpoint at 767px

Scope Boundaries

What's Out of Scope

Be ready to redirect scope creep back to this list. Changes go through Dean Happe.

  • evdiomessage-archive.org and pre-2019 content
  • Booking / reservation functionality (Sarto Retreat Center)
  • Third-party logins or gated member access
  • Email marketing integration (Flocknote) — future phase
  • Searchable digital staff/clergy directory (PDF only)
  • Google Sites / MyDio portal — link-off only
  • Custom event registration or payment processing
  • School, Catholic Charities, or affiliate websites
  • Any integrations beyond OSV (linking only)
  • Analytics dashboards or monthly reporting
  • Google Business Profile management
  • Diocesan Yearbook (link to existing PDF)
  • Login-gated content of any kind
  • Employment application systems (listings only)
