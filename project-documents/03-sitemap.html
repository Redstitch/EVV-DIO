Global Elements (Present on Every Page)

Header

Diocese logo/crest, main navigation (About, Parishes & Mass, Faith & Sacraments, Ministries, Events, The Message), Give button, Search, Spanish language toggle

Utility Nav

MyDio, Careers, Contact, Safe Environment links. EN/ES toggle. Visible on desktop, absorbed into mobile drawer.

Footer

Diocese address & phone, quick links, social media (Facebook, X, Instagram, YouTube), The Message subscribe, privacy, accessibility

Site Search

In utility nav (desktop) and mobile header. Full-site covering pages, PDFs, and articles.

Give CTA

Persistent "Give" button in header on desktop and mobile. Links to OSV.

Breadcrumbs

Contextual breadcrumb navigation on all interior pages for wayfinding.
