Pastoral Priorities 2025–2030
The Message · Feature

A five-year plan, one family

Bishop Siegel announces Pastoral Priorities for 2025–2030 — a diocesan-wide plan centered on the themes of Encounter, Deepen, and Engage.

Across forty-six parishes, twenty-six schools, and every ministry of the diocese, the plan marks the first comprehensive pastoral roadmap since 2017 and asks every Catholic in Southwestern Indiana to take one concrete step this year.
