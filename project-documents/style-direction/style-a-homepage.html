The mission entrusted to all the baptized

We, the Catholics of the Diocese of Evansville, are committed to continuing the mission Jesus entrusted to all the baptized.

To joyfully proclaim the Gospel, serve those in need, and form disciples — inviting everyone to encounter, deepen, and engage.

We are forty-six parishes, twenty-six Catholic schools, and hundreds of ministries — stewards of a faith passed down in this place for more than eighty years.
